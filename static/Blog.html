    What is House Facks?

    House Facks is an On-line Home Inspection Reporting Program that is easy to use, inexpensive, and produces great looking professional reports. Our system costs only $5.00 per report with no downloads, contracts or time limits. Get 10 free home inspection reports at signup.

    You can be up and running in a few minutes with our “step by step” tutorials. “How To” videos located in the video library makes writing a report quick and easy. To help home inspectors stay organized we have included tools like an Appointment Calendar, a reminder system, and a client manager database.

    This Home Inspection system helps you turn a 'One Time Client' into a 'Life Time Customer'. Generate residual income by keeping your customer’s inspection report current with check-up inspections.
